Accenture Innovation Challenge

In this project, teams were required to design and pitch an idea to Habit for Humanity in which we help them get millenials more involved in their project. Our design was to implement an application that made it easy for users to see where Habitat projects are happening, and have a level based incentive system for completing more projects. The overall pitched idea recieved second place in the University wide competition.

Pictured here is the final mockup presented to the judges. It puts an emphasis on the location feature which will allow for easier sign up, and displays the side menu with the options that we will offer on the application. The mockup was created in Figma.
