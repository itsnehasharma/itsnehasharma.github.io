Hi there!

I'm Neha, and I'm a senior at the University of Florida (Go Gators!) majoring in Computer Science and minoring in Sociology. I'm passionate about developing products through the lens of human-centered design.

When I was 13 years old, my dad called me downstairs to show me this episode of 60 minutes. Since then, I've been obsessed with the concept of design thinking. As an engineer, designer, and human being, I'm discovering how to intertwine innovative tech, culture, and the needs of society. Using this knowledge, I want to pioneer amazing products that make an impact on the world in a tangible and creative way.

My goal as an engineer is to constantly be expanding my creative mindset and improving my technical ability in order to produce groundbreaking results.
